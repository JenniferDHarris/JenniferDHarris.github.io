Today we reviewed David Shivar's sermon on "The Joy of Forgiveness"

King David expresses in Psalm 32 that happiness is not something to be sought, happienss is a byproduct of knowing God which comes through the forgiveness of sins.

David sought God, embraced the gospel, and found true joy in trusting and following the Lord.

Isn't it reassuring that when we come to God and confess our sins, He will delight to forgive us?
