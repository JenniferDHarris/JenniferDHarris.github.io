The Joy of Forgiveness

← Back to Summaries

Today we reviewed David Shivar's sermon titled "The Joy of Forgiveness".

King David expresses in Psalm 32 that happiness is not something to be sought; happiness is a byproduct of knowing God, which comes through the forgiveness of sins.

David sought God, embraced the gospel, and found true joy in trusting and following the Lord.

  • Forgiveness is transferring your demands for justice to God for him to execute justice in his time and way.
  • Forgive others as God forgave you. We have been forgiven for infinitely more than we will ever be called to forgive others for.
  • Forgiveness is not optional for Christians; it is a direct command to reflect God’s love and mercy toward others. "... as the Lord has forgiven you, so you also must forgive." (Colossians 3:13b)

Isn't it reassuring that when we come to God and confess our sins, He will delight to forgive us?
